About CycleFit Planner

Helping recreational cyclists improve their health, performance, and consistency through personalized cycling training plans.

Community Statistics

Project Purpose

CycleFit Planner was created to help recreational and amateur cyclists organize their training in a practical and personalized way. Users can generate cycling plans based on their goals, calculate BMI, estimate calories burned, and save training plans locally in their browser.

Main Features

  • Personalized cycling training plans
  • BMI calculation
  • Calories burned estimation
  • Hydration recommendations
  • Plan storage using Local Storage
  • Responsive design
  • Dark mode support
  • Interactive dashboard

Technologies Used

HTML5

Semantic structure, accessibility, and content organization.

CSS3

Responsive layouts using Flexbox, Grid, animations, and components.

JavaScript

Dynamic content, event handling, calculations, and DOM interaction.

Local Storage

Persistent storage of cycling plans directly inside the browser.

How It Works

  1. Enter your personal information.
  2. Select your cycling goal.
  3. Choose your weekly riding time.
  4. Generate your personalized plan.
  5. Review recommendations and statistics.
  6. Save your plan for future use.
